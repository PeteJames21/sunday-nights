He stands, arms folded,\
the forest alive around him.\
Sunlight filters through the green,\
but he's calm, rooted,\
like he belongs here.

Maybe he's thinking,\
maybe just breathing\
but in this moment,\
he's part of the trees,\
and they, a part of him.
