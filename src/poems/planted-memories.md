---
title: Planted Memories
author: Ryan NKP
date: 2025-01-15
tags:
  - Life
---
There are memories I planted
deep enough to forget,
but they sprout anyway,
breaking through the soil of silence.
Some grow thorns,
some bloom softly,
some rise like weeds
I wish I never watered.
But all of them remind me
that the past doesn’t stay dead—
it learns to breathe underground.
