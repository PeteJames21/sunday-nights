title: Just Know I Love you
author: Lihanda
date: 2026-03-04

When you smile, my heart flutters.
Seeing your face, joy captures me—
so deeply I cannot escape it.
*Just know I love you.*

My knees weaken.
My focus dissolves.
With only a glimpse of you,
my body forgets its owner.
*Just know I love you*.

Though I know it was all but a dream,
I never truly had a chance.
My world shatters
when she stands by your side.
I swallow the ache
before it spills into tears.
*Still—just know I love you.*

But this is my farewell.
I have decided to let you go.
I choose to love myself more,
because loving you turned me into a
shell of myself.
