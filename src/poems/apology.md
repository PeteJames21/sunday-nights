"You made me act
that way," he says.

"If you didn't say that,
I wouldn't have done it to you.'

"I'm sorry.
I didn't mean to."

Look—
I hit you,
but you just made
me so angry.
Don't anger me like
that again, okay?"

"I brought you flowers.
And chocolate.
I'm sorry.

Silly little me—
I fell for it.
