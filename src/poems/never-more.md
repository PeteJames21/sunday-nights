---
title: Never More
date: "2024-08-02"
author: Bencity
tags:
  - Love
---

Isn’t it amazing,
That a burgeoning rose,
Or a magical sunset
Or a sweet song’s tune,
Or a full moon’s splendor –
Just the thought of sheer beauty:
Takes each of use –
With the certainty of a compass –
To just one person,
Or a place
And never more!
